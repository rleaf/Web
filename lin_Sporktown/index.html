The Sporktown Scoop

Many people can’t believe there is a town named after a tool that is combination spoon and fork. The truth is the town is named after its founder James T. Spork. He founded the town using the wealth from his invention of the plastic Spork. Few people know it was actually his own last name that inspired the idea of the famous spoon fork combination!

From Humble Beginnings

If James T. Spork were still alive today he’d be amazed to see how the town has grown. What started as a few homes and a small factory, is now a bustling city and cultural center. While Sporktown has changed, at its heart we have kept true to Mr. Spork’s vision. We must never forget our origin and keep the innovation, warmth and honesty that filled his heart.

World Class Cuisine

From comfort food like hot dogs or macaroni and cheese to fine French bistros, Sushi and Sashimi, Sporktown has a restaurant to suit your tastes. Don’t worry, you can eat with chop sticks or whatever you like. Sporks are encouraged, but not required. A town named after an eating utensil has to deliver in the food department, or else we’ll be cooked alive!
